RailApp
Assisting Rail Maintenance & Construction

RailApp is designed to assist professionals in the rail industry with a variety of tasks, including:

  • Calculation of materials for projects
  • Scoping and inspections
  • Rail stress adjustments and destressing
  • Thermal expansion calculations
  • Project management tools
  • Tracking and safety features

With RailApp, you can streamline your rail maintenance and construction operations with ease and precision.
